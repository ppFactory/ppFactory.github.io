VomiPrivacyPolicy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Vomi (“we,” “our,” or “us”) handles your personal information when you use our voice diary application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Important: Vomi is designed with privacy as a core principle. We want to be completely transparent about what we do NOT do:

  • No Permanent Data Storage: We do not permanently store any of your voice recordings, diary entries, or personal data on external servers
  • No User Accounts: We do not require or create user accounts that could track your identity
  • No Analytics: We do not collect usage analytics, crash reports, or device information
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We do not integrate with third-party analytics or advertising services
  • No Personal Identifiers: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personal identifiers

3. How Vomi Works

3.1 Data Processing

Vomi operates with a hybrid approach to protect your privacy:

  • Voice recordings are stored locally in your device’s storage
  • AI emotional analysis requires internet connectivity to access DeepSeek’s AI service
  • Statistical analysis and mood tracking are computed and stored locally
  • Your data is only transmitted when you specifically request AI analysis

3.2 Data Storage

Your personal data is stored:

  • Voice Recordings: In your device’s local storage
  • Emotional Analysis: In your device’s local storage
  • Mood Statistics: In your device’s local storage
  • App Settings: In your device’s local storage

4. AI Analysis and Privacy

4.1 DeepSeek AI Integration

The emotional analysis feature:

  • Requires internet connectivity to access DeepSeek’s AI service
  • Temporarily transmits your voice content for analysis
  • Uses DeepSeek’s advanced AI models for accurate emotional recognition
  • Does not permanently store your content on external servers

4.2 What AI Analyzes

The AI analysis:

  • Processes only the voice content you choose to analyze
  • Provides emotional insights for personal reflection
  • Does not create profiles or track patterns across users
  • Cannot access data from other users
  • Is performed through secure API calls to DeepSeek

5. Data Security

5.1 Device Security

Your data security depends on:

  • Your device’s security settings (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)
  • Your device’s encryption features
  • Your iOS version and security updates
  • Your device backup practices

5.2 App Security

Vomi implements:

  • Local data encryption where possible
  • Secure local storage practices
  • Secure API communication with DeepSeek
  • No permanent external data storage

6. Third-Party Services

Vomi integrates with DeepSeek’s AI service for emotional analysis. This integration:

  • Only occurs when you specifically request AI analysis
  • Uses secure API communication
  • Does not grant DeepSeek permanent access to your data
  • Is necessary for providing the AI emotional analysis feature

7. Children’s Privacy

Vomi is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Data Retention

8.1 Local Data

Your data remains on your device:

  • Until you manually delete it
  • Until you uninstall the App
  • Until your device is reset or replaced

8.2 Limited External Processing

We do not:

  • Create remote backups of your data
  • Store your data in cloud services
  • Access your device’s backup files
  • Permanently store your content on DeepSeek’s servers

9. Your Rights

Since we don’t collect or store your personal data externally, you have complete control over:

  • Your voice recordings
  • Your emotional analysis data
  • Your mood statistics
  • Your app settings

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the App and will not affect the fundamental privacy principles outlined here.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through the App’s support features.

12. International Users

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of Vomi, regardless of their location. When you use AI analysis features, your data may be processed by DeepSeek’s servers, which may be located in different jurisdictions. We ensure that all data transmission follows secure protocols and privacy standards.

Your privacy is our priority. Vomi is designed to keep your personal thoughts and feelings secure, with AI analysis only occurring when you specifically request it through secure DeepSeek integration.
