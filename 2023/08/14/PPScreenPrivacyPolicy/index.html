PPScreen Privacy Policy

Thank you for using the PPScreen application! This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our application. Please read and agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy before you start using the services we provide.
We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. During your use of this application, we may collect and use your health data, location data, and usage data. We will collect, use, and process your personal information within the scope defined by this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy applies to all users of this application.
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If we make any significant changes to this Privacy Policy, we will provide notifications through this application. Please review this Privacy Policy periodically to understand how we protect your personal information.

Explanation of Screen Time Data Usage

PPScreen utilizes Apple’s Screen Time API, allowing users to disable apps during designated time periods. PPScreen displays a list of apps installed on your device and enables you to choose which ones to disable. Please note that the screen time data used by PPScreen is encrypted, and we do not access or store any related data.
You can learn more on Apple’s official documentation page:
https://developer.apple.com/documentation/deviceactivity。

Data Collection and Usage

Health

PPScreen utilizes Apple’s HealthKit API to read and/or write certain data to the Apple Health app, in order to provide application functionalities.
If you do not agree to PPScreen accessing your health data, please refrain from using this feature within the application. If you wish to prevent PPScreen from accessing your health data, you can revoke our access permission within the Apple Health application.
We will not use your health data for marketing, advertising, or purposes such as usage-based data mining, including by third parties. Your health data is accessed locally through Apple Health and will not be stored or cached by the application. Your health data is processed locally and will not be sent to any servers.

Usage Behavior Data

PPScreen utilizes Firebase Analytics to collect usage behavior data that cannot be traced back to your individual identity, helping us enhance and improve our services. We only use the gathered information for researching and enhancing the overall performance of PPScreen.
For more detailed information about Firebase Analytics’ privacy policy, please visit:
https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy。

Crash Data

PPScreen utilizes Firebase Crashlytics to collect application crash data that cannot be traced back to your individual identity. We will use crash data to fix errors and enhance our services.
You can visit https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy for more information about the privacy policy of Firebase Crashlytics.

Data Protection

We use the Firebase SDK to collect production interaction and crash data. The data we collect is anonymous and not associated with users.
We have implemented some of the unmet SDK features, and we do not collect any of your sensitive data. https://www.umeng.com/page/policy

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We do not use cookies or other tracking technologies.

Privacy Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of any changes by posting a new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us at ppfactory001@gmail.com.
